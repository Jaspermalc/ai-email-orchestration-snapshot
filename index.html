AI-Driven Email Orchestration

Build an AI-driven orchestration and content system that coordinates revenue communications across sales, product, and programmatic email — so the right message reaches the right Gustomer at the right time, with AI generating content and Iterable orchestrating delivery.

Owner Revenue Systems Author Sean Counceller Timeframe May 1, 2026 → Apr 30, 2027 Growth — Existing Customer

Goals

  1. Speed. Reduce campaign time-to-live from 2–3 weeks to ≤5 days; first products live in Iterable within 3 months; full Phase 1 with UCP governance by August.
  2. Operational efficiency. Shift team capacity from manual production (drafting, briefs, Marketo config) toward strategy, experimentation, and performance optimization.
  3. Sales & email alignment. Every programmatic email targets the same audiences and products as sales prioritization and reinforces the same messaging.
  4. Customer experience. Consistent, personalized communication across channels; every send governed by UCP for suppression, frequency, and prioritization.

Revenue Systems · Sales Orchestration

Sales Opportunity Ranker

A ranking system that decides which opportunities from Hightouch audiences are prioritized for Sales. The most impactful audiences are surfaced automatically, so App and PMM teams can launch and test new audiences without manual triage — and Sales gets the right opportunity at the right moment.

Owner Revenue Systems DRI Sean Counceller Timeframe Mar 1, 2026 → Aug 1, 2026 Status On Track · FY27 Q2 delivery Growth — Existing Customer

Goals

  1. Scale past manual capacity. Algorithmically prioritize triggers and audiences so Sales can absorb far more opportunities than the team can manually triage.
  2. Surface highest-impact audiences. Route the right opportunity to the right sales team (Expansion, HI, Retirement, Indirect, Direct) based on conversion targets.
  3. Unblock self-serve audiences. App / PMM teams launch and test new audiences without engineering or Sales prioritization overhead.
  4. Measure incrementality. Provide the feedback loop to attribute lift to each audience and trigger, feeding the model back into the orchestration platform.
